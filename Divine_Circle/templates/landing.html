Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How does a virtual pooja work?

Our pandits perform the ceremony live from temples or sacred locations in India. You join via HD video call and can participate, ask questions, and receive blessings in real-time. The experience is as authentic as being there in person.

Do I need any materials (samagri) for the pooja?

We provide a complete list of required items before the ceremony. You can either purchase them locally or opt for our samagri delivery service, where we ship everything you need directly to your doorstep. Basic items like flowers, incense, and fruits are typically available locally.

Are the pandits qualified and experienced?

Yes! All our pandits are background-verified with decades of experience performing traditional Vedic rituals. They come from respected families of priests and are well-versed in Sanskrit, mantras, and ceremonial procedures.

What if I'm in a different time zone?

We work around your schedule! Our team will coordinate with you to find a suitable time that works for both you and the pandit. We also calculate auspicious muhurtas adjusted for your local timezone.

How long does a typical pooja take?

It varies by ceremony. Simple poojas take 30-45 minutes, while elaborate ones like Satyanarayan Katha can take 2-3 hours. We'll inform you of the expected duration when you book.

Can family members from different locations join?

Absolutely! Multiple family members from different countries can join the same ceremony via video call. It's a beautiful way to bring the family together spiritually.

What happens after the pooja is completed?

You'll receive a recorded video of the entire ceremony, high-quality photos, and blessed prasad shipped to your address internationally. The recording is yours to keep and share with family.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes. If you cancel 48 hours before the scheduled time, you'll receive a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours receive a 50% refund. We also offer rescheduling at no additional cost.

Can I customize the pooja according to my family traditions?

Definitely! We encourage you to share your family's specific traditions and customs. Our pandits will accommodate regional variations and personal preferences to make the ceremony truly yours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and international bank transfers. Payment is secure and processed through encrypted channels.
